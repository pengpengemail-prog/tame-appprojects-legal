TAME SlimVideo Privacy Policy

TAME SlimVideo is designed as a local-first video compression utility.

Video Scan

When you start a scan, the app reads video details on device to show large files worth reviewing, estimate storage savings, and let you choose what to compress. Compressed copies are saved back only when you choose Save Compressed Copy, and an original video is removed only after you explicitly choose that action.

Local Processing

Video review, compression, and saved-space calculations run on device. TAME SlimVideo does not require an account and does not upload your videos to a server for compression.

Tracking and Advertising

TAME SlimVideo does not use advertising SDKs, third-party tracking, cross-app tracking, or data sale.

Your Control

You decide whether to scan videos, which videos to compress, whether to save a compressed copy, and whether to remove the original. If the system does not provide video details, the app cannot build the local review list or save compressed results.
