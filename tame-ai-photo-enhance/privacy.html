TAME AI Photo Enhance Privacy Policy

TAME AI Photo Enhance uses photo library access only for images you choose to enhance. Image processing runs locally on the device.

We do not sell personal data, do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and do not track users across apps or websites.

Selected images remain under your control unless you choose to share or export them through system actions.
