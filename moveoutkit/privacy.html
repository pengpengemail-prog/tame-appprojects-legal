Privacy Policy — MoveOut Kit

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Summary

  • MoveOut Kit has no account and no custom server.
  • Your packs and evidence photos are stored locally on your device.
  • The app collects no analytics, shows no ads, and does not track you.

What we store

All pack content — addresses, names, section statuses, notes, and photos — is stored on-device (a local JSON snapshot and SwiftData). Photos live in the app's private storage. This data is included only in your own encrypted device backups; it is never sent to us.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data. We operate no servers that receive your content.

Support email

If you email support, your message contains only the details you choose to include.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and the published version.
