Support — MoveOut Kit

Contact

Email support@moveoutkit.app with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. We typically reply within a few business days.

FAQ

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. MoveOut Kit has no account and no cloud server. Packs and photos never leave your iPhone.

What sections are included? Rooms photographed, Cleaning completed, Keys/remotes returned, Utilities/meter photo, Damage notes, and Final notes. Mark each Done, Needs attention, or Not applicable.

What's in the exported PDF? The address and handoff details, each checklist section with its status and notes, your photos, and any typed acknowledgement.

Does this guarantee my deposit back? No. MoveOut Kit is a personal record. It does not guarantee any deposit outcome and is not legal advice.

Is there a subscription? No. One-time purchase, no in-app purchases, no ads.
