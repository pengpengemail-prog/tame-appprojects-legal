TAME Secure Hide Privacy Policy

TAME Secure Hide is a local file privacy utility. It does not require an account, does not track users, and does not sell or share personal data.

Files selected for encryption or decryption are processed on device. The app does not upload selected files to a cloud service.

Passwords are not recoverable by the app. Keep your password safe.
