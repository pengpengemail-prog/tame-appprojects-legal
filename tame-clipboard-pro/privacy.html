TAME Clipboard Pro Privacy Policy

TAME Clipboard Pro stores clipboard history and quick phrases locally on your device for user-visible copy and paste workflows.

We do not sell personal data, do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and do not track users across apps or websites.

Clipboard content remains local unless you choose to share it through system actions.
