TAME QR Scanner Privacy Policy

TAME QR Scanner uses camera access only to scan QR codes and barcodes. Scan and generated history is stored locally on your device.

We do not sell personal data, do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and do not track users across apps or websites.

Camera frames are used for code detection in the app workflow.
