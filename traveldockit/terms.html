What the app is

TravelDoc Kit is a personal organizer for travel documents and a pre-trip checklist. It stores your documents locally, surfaces OCR suggestions, masks sensitive values on screen, schedules local reminders, and exports a PDF. It is a one-time paid download with no subscription and no in-app purchases.

What the app is not

TravelDoc Kit does not:

  • provide visa, immigration, or entry-requirement advice;
  • guarantee travel safety;
  • recommend or sell insurance;
  • verify the authenticity of any official document;
  • check flight status or scan your email or calendar.

OCR suggestions are convenience estimates you must review. Always confirm entry, visa, and insurance requirements with official sources before you travel.
