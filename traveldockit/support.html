Support — TravelDoc Kit

Contact

Email support@traveldockit.app with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. We typically reply within a few business days.

FAQ

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. TravelDoc Kit has no account and no cloud server, and it does not scan your email or calendar. Your documents never leave your iPhone.

What does privacy mode do? It hides sensitive values (passport, visa, insurance) on screen so you can show your checklist without exposing numbers. Your data still lives locally on your device.

Does the scanner upload my documents? No. OCR runs on-device with Apple's Vision framework. Suggestions are generated locally and nothing is uploaded.

What's in the exported PDF? The trip details and the checklist with each document's status and a count of attached copies. Sensitive document numbers are not printed.

Does it check visas or flight status? No. TravelDoc Kit only organizes your documents. Confirm entry, visa, and insurance requirements with official sources.

Is there a subscription? No. One-time purchase, no in-app purchases, no ads.
