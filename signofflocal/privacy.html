Privacy Policy — Signoff Local

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Summary

  • Signoff Local has no account and no custom server.
  • Your records, photos, and signature images are stored locally on your device.
  • The app collects no analytics, shows no ads, and does not track you.

What we store

All signoff content — titles, dates, locations, parties, notes, photos, and signature images — is stored on-device (a local JSON snapshot and SwiftData). Images live in the app's private storage. This data is included only in your own encrypted device backups; it is never sent to us.

Signatures

Signatures are captured on-device and saved as local images, then flattened into your exported PDF. They are simple acknowledgements; we do not verify identity and keep no audit trail.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data. We operate no servers that receive your content.

Support email

If you email support, your message contains only the details you choose to include.
