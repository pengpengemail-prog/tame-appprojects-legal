TAME Storage Analyzer Privacy Policy

TAME Storage Analyzer reviews local file metadata and device-accessible folders to present storage insights such as large files, cache size, and potential duplicate image groups.

We do not sell personal data, do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and do not track users across apps or websites.

Storage analysis runs locally on the device.
