TAME 商业起名 Support

TAME 商业起名是一款本地运行的商业起名参考工具，支持公司名、店铺名、产品名、品牌名、候选名保存和字库检索。

Version 1.0 is free to download and offers optional auto-renewable Pro subscriptions through the App Store. Pro unlocks candidate-name saving and ongoing comparison. The app does not include external purchase links, developer-hosted payments, accounts, or advertising.

Reviewer path

  • No demo account is required.
  • Open the app and enter a company, shop, product, or brand name on the main naming screen.
  • Tap the primary action to view fit, rationale, and suggested adjustment direction.
  • Open Pro from Settings or the save prompt to verify StoreKit products, purchase, and restore-purchase flow.
  • Use the Character Library, Saved Candidates, and Settings tabs to review local reference data, support notes, and privacy notes.

Support scope

  • Main features can be used without account registration.
  • Name, brand, and preference analysis uses bundled local reference data.
  • The app does not collect user data, use advertising SDKs, or perform cross-app tracking.
  • Pro is subscription-based. Subscription purchase, cancellation, renewal, restore, and refund handling are managed by Apple through the App Store.

Important boundary

Results are for cultural reference, naming inspiration, and structured comparison only. They are not legal, trademark, medical, financial, or business-success advice, and they do not promise name approval, trademark availability, domain availability, or any guaranteed outcome.
