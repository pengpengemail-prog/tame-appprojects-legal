TAME 名扬天下 Support

TAME 名扬天下是一款本地运行的姓名分析与起名参考工具，支持姓名测算、个人起名、品牌名称分析和字库检索。

Version 1.0 is a paid-upfront App Store app. Users buy the app once through the App Store. The app itself does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, external purchase links, accounts, or additional payment flows.

Reviewer path

  • No demo account is required.
  • Open the app and use the default example on the main analysis screen.
  • Use the analysis-mode dropdown on the home screen to switch between personal name analysis, naming suggestions, number analysis, and brand-name analysis.
  • Use the Character Library, Data, and Settings tabs to review local reference data, support notes, and privacy notes.

Support scope

  • Main features can be used without account registration.
  • Name, brand, and preference analysis uses bundled local reference data.
  • The app does not collect user data, use advertising SDKs, or perform cross-app tracking.
  • Current China mainland customer price target is CNY 88 as an App Store paid download.

Important boundary

Results are for cultural reference, naming inspiration, and structured comparison only. They are not legal, trademark, medical, financial, or business-success advice, and they do not promise any guaranteed outcome.
