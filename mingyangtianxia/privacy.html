TAME 商业起名 Privacy Policy

TAME 商业起名 is a local-first business naming reference utility. The app is designed for cultural reference, naming inspiration, candidate saving, and organized comparison.

Data collection

  • Version 1.0 does not collect user personal data.
  • The main app experience does not require account registration, phone verification, or third-party login.
  • The app does not use advertising SDKs, analytics attribution SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
  • Name, brand, and preference analysis is processed with bundled local reference data.

Payments

  • The app is free to download and offers optional auto-renewable Pro subscriptions processed by Apple through the App Store.
  • Pro subscriptions unlock candidate-name saving and ongoing comparison features. Available product options may include monthly and yearly plans.
  • The app does not include developer-hosted payment processing, external purchase links, advertising purchases, or a developer account system.
  • App Store purchases, subscription management, refunds, and receipt handling are managed by Apple.

Connectivity and diagnostics

  • Main features can be used without network access.
  • If Apple system diagnostics are provided, they are used only to improve stability.
  • If a future version adds online services, account features, or data sync, this privacy policy will be updated.
