TAME FileMaster Privacy Policy

TAME FileMaster is designed as a local utility. The app processes files selected or accessible on your device for browsing, compression, extraction, PDF tools, batch rename, and QR generation.

We do not sell personal data, do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and do not track users across apps or websites.

Files remain on your device unless you choose to share or export them through system actions.
