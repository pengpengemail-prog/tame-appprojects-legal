Support — PropertyProof

Contact

Email support@propertyproof.app with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. We typically reply within a few business days.

FAQ

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. PropertyProof has no account and no cloud server. Reports and photos never leave your iPhone.

Can I add my own rooms? Yes. Each report starts with a standard room set; you can add custom rooms, rename, or remove rooms per report.

What's in the exported PDF? The property address and details, each room with its status and notes, the photos you captured, and any typed acknowledgement.

Is this a legal inspection or proof? No. PropertyProof is a personal photo record. It is not a certified inspection and does not guarantee any deposit or dispute outcome.

How do I move my data to a new phone? Restore your new device from an encrypted backup made under your Apple ID; the app's local data is included. You can also export individual reports as PDFs.

Is there a subscription? No. One-time purchase, no in-app purchases, no ads.
