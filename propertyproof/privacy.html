Privacy Policy — PropertyProof

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Summary

  • PropertyProof has no account and no custom server.
  • Your reports and room photos are stored locally on your device.
  • The app collects no analytics, shows no ads, and does not track you.

What we store

All report content — addresses, names, room statuses, notes, and photos — is stored on-device using the system's local storage (a local JSON snapshot and SwiftData). Photos live in the app's private storage area. This data is included only in your own encrypted device backups; it is never sent to us.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data. We operate no servers that receive your content.

Support email

If you email support, your message contains only the details you choose to include. We use it solely to help you and do not share it.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and the version published with the app.
