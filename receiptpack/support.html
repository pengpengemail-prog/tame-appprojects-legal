Support — ReceiptPack

Contact

Email support@receiptpack.app with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. We typically reply within a few business days.

FAQ

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. ReceiptPack has no account, no cloud server, and no bank connection. Your receipts never leave your iPhone.

Does the scanner send my receipts anywhere? No. Text recognition runs on-device with Apple's Vision framework. Suggestions are generated locally and nothing is uploaded.

How do packs work? Switch the Packs tab between "By month" and "By project". Each pack shows a running total and exports to PDF or CSV.

Does ReceiptPack do my taxes or find deductions? No. ReceiptPack only organizes receipts. It does not provide tax advice, recommend deductions, optimize refunds, or file taxes. Please consult a qualified professional.

Is there a subscription? No. One-time purchase, no in-app purchases, no ads.
