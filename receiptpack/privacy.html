Privacy Policy — ReceiptPack

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Summary

  • ReceiptPack has no account, no custom server, and no bank connection.
  • Your receipts, images, and expense details are stored locally on your device.
  • The app collects no analytics, shows no ads, and does not track you.

What we store

All receipt content — merchants, dates, totals, categories, projects, notes, and images — is stored on-device (a local JSON snapshot and SwiftData). Images live in the app's private storage. This data is included only in your own encrypted device backups; it is never sent to us.

Text recognition (OCR)

When you attach a receipt image, ReceiptPack can suggest the merchant, date, and total. This recognition runs entirely on your device with Apple's Vision framework. Images and recognized text are never uploaded.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, location, or banking data. We operate no servers that receive your content.

Support email

If you email support, your message contains only the details you choose to include.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and the published version.
